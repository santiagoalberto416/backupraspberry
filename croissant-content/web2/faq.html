What´s FAQ?

Frequently asked questions (FAQ) or Questions and Answers (Q&A), are listed questions and answers, all supposed to be commonly asked in some context, and pertaining to a particular topic. The format is commonly used on email mailing lists and other online forums, where certain common questions tend to recur. "FAQ" is pronounced as either an initialism (F-A-Q) or an acronym. Since the acronym FAQ originated in textual media, its pronunciation varies; "F-A-Q", and "fack", are commonly heard. Depending on usage, the term may refer specifically to a single frequently asked question, or to an assembled list of many questions and their answers. Web page designers often label a single list of questions as a "FAQ", such as on Google.com, while using "FAQs" to denote multiple lists of questions such as on United States Treasury site

FAQ For Assistants

An Esencial part of our users are the Assistants who are involved on our conferences and panels; These suggestions are focused to give an easy experience on the platform Croissant.

How to Ask A Question
  1. Log in Into the Croissant Platform.
  2. Select the Conference where you´re involved.
  3. Select the Button, "ASK".
  4. Write Your Question About the Conference.
  5. Select the Button "SEND".
What I Have to Do if I Exceed the Three Questions?
  1. Relax.
  2. Wait three minutes* to get another three questions.
  3. Keep Asking Your Doubts.

* The time will be determinated by the administrator of the conference.

How Can I Play Trivia?

Play Trivia is one of the easiest ways to get some rewards from the conference, and also proof your knowledges about the conference

  1. Log in Into the Croissant Platform.
  2. Select the Conference where you´re involved.
  3. Press the "PLAY TRIVIA" Button.
  4. Choose The Correct Answer.
  5. Relax & Have Fun!.
